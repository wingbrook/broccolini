About me

A student at NAIT

Hello, my name is not really Wing Broccolini and I am passionate about learning different ways of preparing and enjoying broccolini. I've experimented with all kinds of Broccolis in so many ways, and honestly, it's one of the most versatile veggies you'll find. From grilling, steaming, to salting and even including it in a salad, it has become a household vegetable in my kitchen.

This is because what makes me most excited about broccolini is how easy it is to work with in almost every meal. It is healthy, tasty and can really enhance the flavor of any meal. On this site, I am going to be sharing some basic recipes that will help you include broccolini in your daily meals and eat healthy no matter what your cooking skills are.

I have spent a lot of time developing recipes that are not only good for you but also bursting with flavour. If you are in search of a quick and easy dinner idea or want to come up with something special, these recipes will help you to understand that broccolini can be really tasty.

Are you in the mood for some exciting meals? You’ve come to the right place! I’m excited to introduce you to these recipes and I’m sure you’ll learn to love broccolini as much as I do. Let’s hop into the kitchen and have some fun with it!
